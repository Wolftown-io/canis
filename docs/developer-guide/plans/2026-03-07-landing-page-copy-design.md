# Landing Page Copy Redesign

**Date:** 2026-03-07
**Status:** Approved

## Context

The landing page design/layout is finalized. The text needs a rewrite to align with Kaiku's target audience (gamers and privacy-conscious users) and voice guidelines.

## Voice Guidelines

- **Tone:** Mix of straight-talking gamer friend, confident indie project, and plain-language technical credibility
- **Language:** Easy to understand, no jargon, no corporate/marketing speak
- **Honesty:** No false promises (e.g., "zero latency"), acknowledge development status
- **Audience:** Gamers and privacy-concerned people who know nothing about Kaiku
- **Positioning:** Why Kaiku over Discord/TeamSpeak — control, privacy, performance

## Terminology Decision

- Avoid the word "server" entirely to prevent ambiguity between self-hosting infrastructure and community spaces (guilds)
- Use "communities" when referring to guild-like spaces
- Use "host it yourself" when referring to self-hosting

## Approved Copy

### Hero
- **Badge:** Open Source Voice Chat
- **Title:** Your Voice. Your Server. Your Rules.
- **Subtitle:** Kaiku is a free, self-hosted voice and text chat built for gaming. No tracking, no ads, no surprise changes to features you depend on. Just fast, encrypted communication that you control.

### Features Section
- **Heading:** What You Get
- **Subtext:** Built to be fast, private, and out of your way.

1. **Fast Voice** — Your callouts land when they need to, not a second later. Low-latency audio that keeps up with your game.
2. **Your Data, Yours** — Host it yourself. Your messages and calls are end-to-end encrypted. Nobody reads your conversations — not even us.
3. **Lightweight Client** — Built in Rust, so it barely touches your CPU or RAM. Your games get the resources, not your chat app.
4. **Game Presence** — Automatically shows what you and your friends are playing. No manual setup needed.
5. **Screen Sharing** — Share your screen or game with multiple streams at once. Great for strategy talk or just hanging out.
6. **Workspaces** — Pin your favorite channels across different communities into one place. Quick access to the stuff you actually use.

### How It Works Section
- **Heading:** Built With Care
- **Subtext:** The tech behind Kaiku — and what it means for you.

1. **The Interface** / Solid JS — A reactive UI framework that keeps things snappy. No lag when you scroll, switch channels, or open menus — even on older hardware.
2. **The Engine** / Rust & Tauri — The app runs natively on your system instead of inside a browser. That means less RAM, less CPU, and no Electron bloat slowing down your games.
3. **The Voice** / WebRTC & Olm — Industry-standard real-time audio paired with end-to-end encryption. Your voice gets there fast, and nobody can listen in.
